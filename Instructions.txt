To play the game:
1) Click "Play"
2) Select a vehicle class
3) Select your car or motorcycle
4) Moving your mouse left and right, try to dodge the
objects that are coming towards your vehicle! For every object you dodge, you are awarded 1 point! Points are displayed in the top left of the screen under your vehicle name

To watch the computer play:
1) Click "Play"
2) Select the "Car" option
3) Select the "Watch AI" button
